AVATAR

"True wisdom lies not in accumulating vast amounts of knowledge, but in understanding the limitations of one's own understanding. It is the recognition that despite the breadth of our education or the depth of our experience, there is always more to learn, and there are always perspectives we have not yet considered. The wise person embraces humility, knowing that every situation has complexities and nuances that are not immediately apparent. They listen more than they speak, seek to understand before being understood, and approach every challenge with a sense of curiosity and openness.
